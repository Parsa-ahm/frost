# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name
Frost — Cold Call Tracker

## Short description (132 chars max)
Track cold calls from Google Maps. Log outcomes, retry no-answers, and sync qualified leads to your CRM.

## Detailed description
Frost overlays Google Maps business search results with live call status badges so you never lose track of who you've called.

**How it works**
Search for businesses on Google Maps ("plumbers toronto", "electricians mississauga", etc.). Frost adds a small colored dot to every card in the results list. After each call, click the dot and mark the outcome in one tap.

**Outcomes**
- No Answer — yellow dot, surfaced in the Retry tab for follow-up
- Called — blue dot, spoke to someone
- Rejected — red dot, hard no
- Qualified — green dot, interested lead

**Side panel**
Pin the side panel while you work. It shows live session stats, a full call log with timestamps, a Retry tab (all no-answer leads sorted oldest-first — your Friday callback list), and a Stats tab with daily breakdowns.

**CRM integration**
Connect your Twenty CRM instance to push qualified leads with one click. All other leads stay out of your CRM until they earn it.

**Privacy**
All data stays on your device in Chrome's local storage. No accounts, no servers, no telemetry.
